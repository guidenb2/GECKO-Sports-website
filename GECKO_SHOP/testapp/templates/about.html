About Us?

GECKO Sports is a Dublin based start-up setup by 2 full-time students, Ben & Harish. Ben and I are currently in our pen-ultimate years in DCU (Dublin City University) studying Computer Applications. GECKO Sports was not planned nor did we ever want to setup a company together. It was something that spawned out our mutual connections and interests in Sport. Ben participates in professional cross country running with his club Clonliffe Harriers AC while I play Senior Football with my local GAA club, Ballymun Kickhams. Although both of our clubs were completely different, we did however share a common problem, the constant need for equipment and clothing. At the time, there were countless companies that were in a position to suit our needs but they were just way too expensive. There was no company that provided affordable but also high quality material. We saw this as a profitable and demanding market and said "why don't we give it a shot?". Voila!
